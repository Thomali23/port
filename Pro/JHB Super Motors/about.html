About Us

Selected Pre-owned Vehicle's

Jhb Super Motors

We at JHB Super Motors began trading in April 2010, we started small but quickly grew to one of the most prominent second hand car dealerships in the Alberton area. We have a wide range of vehicles, catering to all our clients' needs.

At JHB Super Motors we pride ourselves in customer satisfaction. We understand that purchasing a vehicle is one of the largest investments that a person will make and we strive to provide a unique purchasing experience to each of our individual customers. We pride ourselves in ensuring that the often “unpleasant” and “emotional” experience of purchasing a vehicle is made a memorable and joyful experience, by providing personal attention to each individual that we do business with. We aim to deliver a quality product, backed up by our personal, individualised service and the vast amount of collective experience of our dedicated staff compliment. We at JHB Super Motors are proud of our honesty and integrity when it comes to selling quality products.

At JHB Super Motors we believe that our value for money, excellent after sale service, as well as our professional management and staff are the reason that we will go from strength to strength and that we will live up to the reputation of one of the BEST in this industry!

At JHB Super Motors we take care of our clients, cause after all we are family.
